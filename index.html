Brindisa DSE Self-Assessment – Office & Home (Hybrid)

IMPORTANT – Please read before completing
This DSE self-assessment applies to staff who use display screen equipment daily for continuous periods of an hour or more.

If you work both from home (WFH) and in the office, this assessment must cover each workstation you regularly use. You may complete one assessment covering both locations, or separate assessments if your setups differ significantly.

Complete all sections honestly. For each question select Yes, No, or Action Needed. Any Action Needed responses will be flagged to Facilities, who will review and follow up where required.

GDPR / Data protection: We collect your Brindisa email address and DSE responses for Health & Safety compliance and to follow up any actions. Data is stored securely in Airtable and accessed only by authorised staff.
